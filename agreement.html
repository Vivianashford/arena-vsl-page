1. Scope of Engagement

Arena Business Advisors, LLC ("The Arena Partners" or "Advisor") agrees to provide advisory services under the Transferable Exit System program to the Client named above.

The engagement encompasses a comprehensive, hands-on advisory process designed to systemize, de-personalize, and prepare the Client's business for a premium exit or institutional sale - resulting in a business that operates independently of the owner.

2. Duration

This engagement is structured for a minimum of 18 months from the Effective Date, with monthly review checkpoints and milestone-based progress tracking.

Extension beyond 18 months may be negotiated based on progress and mutual agreement.

3. Fee Structure

Engagement Fee: To be discussed

Payment Structure: An initial deposit is collected upon signing this agreement to secure your engagement and initiate onboarding. The remaining balance is structured as a monthly retainer over the engagement period.

All fees are non-refundable once the engagement has commenced, except as outlined in the Alignment Guarantee below.

4. Engagement Phases

The Transferable Exit System is delivered in four structured phases:

Phase 1
Alignment
Deep discovery, goal calibration, and exit readiness baseline. Identify gaps, set targets, and define the transformation roadmap.
Phase 2
On-Site Immersion
Hands-on, in-person operational audit. Shadow key departments, document processes, and capture institutional knowledge at the source.
Phase 3
System Build
Build the operating system - SOPs, org structure, KPIs, leadership team empowerment - so the business runs without you.
Phase 4
Market Ready
Prepare the full exit package: financial normalization, CIM, buyer positioning, and deal facilitation to maximize enterprise value.
5. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all business information, financial data, processes, strategies, client lists, and proprietary methodologies shared during this engagement.

This obligation survives the termination of this agreement for a period of three (3) years. Neither party shall disclose confidential information to third parties without prior written consent, except as required by law.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate this engagement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, all work product, deliverables, and materials produced to date remain the property of the Client.

Fees paid prior to termination are non-refundable. Monthly retainer payments cease upon the 30-day notice period end date.

7. The Arena Alignment Guarantee

The Arena Partners commits to the following five obligations throughout this engagement:

  • 1 Full Commitment: We dedicate the resources, expertise, and senior-level attention required to achieve your exit goals on the agreed timeline.
  • 2 Radical Transparency: We will tell you what we see - including the hard truths - with no sugarcoating. Our job is to fix the business, not to protect your ego.
  • 3 System-First Approach: Every recommendation will be designed for long-term operability, not quick fixes. We build for the buyer, not for appearances.
  • 4 Access to Network: You will have access to our curated network of M&A advisors, brokers, family offices, and strategic buyers relevant to your business and sector.
  • 5 Satisfaction Checkpoint: At the 90-day mark, if you are not satisfied with the direction, approach, or quality of work, we will refund the most recent month's retainer and part ways on good terms.
8. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Texas. Any disputes arising under this agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Harris County, Texas.
